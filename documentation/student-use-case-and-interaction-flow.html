Use-Case and Interaction-Flow

Precondition: Bob is on the Tech Hub home page

Post-condition: Bob has created his profile

  • Bob clicks on the join button
  • A window opens requesting his email and new password
  • He enters his email, creates a password, and presses submit
  • A message appears notifying Bob to check his email for a code and
    and enter it into the input box below to verify his account
  • Bob enters the code and clicks submit
  • The window closes and Bob is now logged in to his account
  • He then clicks on his name in the upper right of the screen
  • A menu drops down with the options of logging off or going to his account
  • He clicks on go to my account
  • The site goes to his account page
  • Bob clicks on the edit button
  • An editable version of his account page comes up
  • He fills out the various fields and clicks on create profile

Bob has now created his profile
