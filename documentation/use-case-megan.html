Use Case and Interaction Flow

Precondition: Megan is on the Tech Hub home page

Post-condition: Megan has located the post a job link

  • Megan clicks on post a job button.
  • The website opens a page with a form fill in section.
  • Megan begins to fill out the empty form containers.
  • The website lets her fill out forms and gives drop-down boxes for her to chose on certain containers.
  • Megan selects upload image button to upload her company's logo.
  • The website on button click prompts her to upload image from her local computer.
  • Megan finishes the form and clicks save and preview button.
  • The website then allows her to see her data as it will be displayed on the job post itself.
  • Megan then reviews her posting and clicks create posting button.
  • The website saves the data and takes her back to a completed job posting.
